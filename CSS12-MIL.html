What is MIL?

Media and Information Literacy (MIL) helps people critically evaluate media content, identify sources of information, and create responsible media messages. It empowers learners to use media wisely and ethically.

Types of Media with Examples

Print Media

Example: Newspaper (e.g. Philippine Daily Inquirer)

Broadcast Media

Example: Television (e.g. GMA News, CNN)

Digital Media

Example: Social Media (e.g. Facebook, YouTube)

Outdoor Media

Example: Billboard advertisements

Why MIL is Important

  1. Helps identify trustworthy information sources.
  2. Teaches responsible use of media and technology.
  3. Enhances digital citizenship and critical thinking.
  4. Prepares students for active participation in society.
